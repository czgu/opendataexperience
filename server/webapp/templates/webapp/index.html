What is Nutri-Tools' Nutrition Tracker?

According to the Journal of the American Medical Association, more than one third of the American population is obese in 2008. And since then, the obesity rates have only gotten worse. Also, the recommended amount of sodium is around 2000mg per day, but the average intake is about 3400mg. It may be difficult to make healthy choices because we are unaware of what is in our food. However, you have no need to worry! With Nutri-Tools' Nutrition Tracker you can now know what nutrients are in your food! Just record all the food items you had during the day, and this app would be able to let you know whether you're on the right track to a healthy diet.
